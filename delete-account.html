Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Enter your registered GITAM roll number below.
  2. Click on "Request Deletion" to submit your request.
  3. You will receive a confirmation GITAM roll number to verify your request.
  4. Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

Data Retention Policy:

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Personal profile information
  • Usage history and preferences
  • Stored messages and interactions

Some data may be retained for legal and security purposes for up to 30 days.

If you need further assistance, please contact us at support@scorewithchitti.in.
