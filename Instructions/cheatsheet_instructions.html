Cheatsheet — Instructions

Follow the CPS strictly and cover all listed topics.

Structure content as: Unit → Topic → Subtopic.

Focus only on exam-important points: definitions, key formulas, diagrams, common mistakes, and memory tricks.

Keep content concise. Limit definitions to 1–2 lines and avoid long paragraphs.

For each topic include:

  • 4–8 exam-relevant bullet points
  • Formulas in visible boxes using standard terminology
  • High-quality, well-labeled diagrams
  • Tables for comparisons and shortcuts/memory tricks

Prioritize high-weightage topics and frequent PYQs. Do not mix unrelated topics on the same page.

Format the document cleanly—check spacings, consistent text sizes, and font style.

After pushing the content into the app, thoroughly verify the entire subject content assigned to you.
