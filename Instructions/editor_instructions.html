Instructions for Video Editing

Subjects will be assigned by the Admin.

Review raw footage and instructions provided by the Operations Intern.

Use only the approved video editing software (will be informed by the Editing Team Lead).

Access all files through the assigned subject inside the Chitti_Architects app.

Download the required topic video.

Trim unnecessary portions from the start and end of the video.

Add the official intro and outro.

Enhance and normalize audio; remove background noise such as hum or hiss.

Merge multiple clips smoothly only if required and explicitly instructed.

Remove long pauses, fumbles, and dead air.

Ensure video framing remains consistent throughout.

Export the final video in MP4 format (1080p).

Use the same filename format as the source video.

Upload the final processed video back to the same topic in the app.

Cross-check the edited videos carefully before final submission:

  • Audio quality
  • Visual clarity
  • Lip sync
  • Overall pacing
